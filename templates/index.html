The Crisis

When War Erases Your Medical History

Millions of displaced people arrive at hospitals with nothing but memory, damaged documents, and mobile phone photos — in a language doctors don't understand.

📋

Lost Medical Records

Prescriptions, lab reports, and discharge papers left behind or destroyed during evacuation

🌐

Language Barriers

Medical documents in Ukrainian, Arabic, Pashto, Tigrinya, Burmese, or tribal scripts that local healthcare workers cannot read

💊

Medication Continuity

Chronic patients (diabetes, hypertension, asthma) losing track of critical prescriptions

⚠️

Unknown Allergies

Critical allergy information missing, creating life-threatening risks during emergency care
